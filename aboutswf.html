What is SWF?

Secure Water Future is a collaborative of over 80 investigators from across the semi-arid western US aiming to improve agricultural and environmental water resilience.

The teamâ€™s goal is to understand, enable, and envision water management strategies, empowered through data-enabled decision-making, for agricultural and ecological systems in the Southwestern US.

By working with growers, irrigation districts, and ecosystem managers, we are adopting and adapting climate resilience strategies, training the next generation of transdisciplinary practitioners, and producing an online dashboard for data-driven decision-making.
