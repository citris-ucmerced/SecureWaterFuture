Secure Water Future's: Super Soaker Water Academy

July 25th - July 29th, 2022

This is a week-long program that will teach students all about water and how it is stored and used in California. By the end of the week, students will have participated in water activities to teach them the relationship between water, agriculture, climate change, and how we can use technology to help our water systems.
