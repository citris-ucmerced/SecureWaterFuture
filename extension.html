Extension

Extension provides educational resources and assistance to individuals and communities in various fields. The program is typically run by a partnership between federal, state, and local governments, along with universities and other organizations. Extension agents and educators work to deliver research-based knowledge and information to individuals and groups through workshops, classes, and one-on-one consultations. The goal of Extension is to help individuals and communities make informed decisions, improve their lives, and strengthen their communities through education and outreach.
