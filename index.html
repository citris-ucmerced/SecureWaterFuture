Our Approach Research

The SWIM team is focused on four research thrusts:
  • Climate Resilience: Developing Climate Services for Data Driven Decisions
  • Markets: Research and Practice on Water Markets through Economics and Institutions
  • Management: Novel Mechanisms for Changing the Water Balance
  • Measurement : Adding Accuracy to Water Budget Accounting
